Yosemite National Park

About Yosemite:

Yosemite National Park is located in the western Sierra Nevada mountains in California. It is known for its giant sequoia trees, Tunnel View, and the famous El Capitan and Half Down granite cliffs. On average, over four million people visit Yosemite National Park per year. The park is managed by the National Park Service and spans over 748,436 acres.

Top Places to Visit in Yosemite:

  1. Yosemite Valley
  2. The Half Dome Peak
  3. Yosemite Falls
